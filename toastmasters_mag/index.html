Toastmasters Magazine Archive

Toastmasters was founded on 22 October 1924 by Dr. Ralph C. Smedley to help people improve their public speaking and leadership skills.

The Gavel was the first magazine published for Toastmasters members starting in 1930, later renamed to The Toastmaster in April 1933. In 2004, the magazine was rebranded as Toastmasters Magazine to better reflect its purpose and audience. More information about the history of the magazine can be found at toastmasters.org.
